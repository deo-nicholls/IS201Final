Experience and Skills

This is who I am.

I love experience. That is where I grow, where I learn, and where I apply. My greatest experience in my life so far was a 2 year mission trip in Slovakia and the Czech Republic. I learned the Slovak and Czech language, and spent 2 years teaching and serving others as a volunteer. Getting out of America has been the greatest experience of growth in my life. Seeing the world changed my perspective. The world is a book, and those who only see 1 country only read 1 page of it.

My time in Slovakia and the Czech Republic led to a study abroad in Europe, where I visited London, Paris and Rome while studying Marketing, Accounting and Global Supply Chain. This added even more understanding of the world, and specifically the world of business. It was another life changing experience.

In addition to those skills that I learned as a teacher for 2 years, and the communication skills I learned from that, and the business mindset I gained from the study abroad, I have spent my free time in working hard towards a goal I have had for almost a decade: The Kona Ironman Triathlon. I am a triathlete, and I have gained a powerful sense of dedication and focus by working towards that difficult goal. An Ironman is no small feat, but since when has life been about doing what's easy?
